CREDIT RISK POLICY DOCUMENT

1. Credit Default Definition:
A credit default occurs when a borrower fails to make scheduled payments for more than 90 consecutive days.

2. Risk Classification:
Customers are classified into three risk categories:
- Low Risk: Credit score above 750
- Medium Risk: Credit score between 600 and 750
- High Risk: Credit score below 600

3. Loan Approval Criteria:
- Minimum income requirement: ₹30,000 per month
- Debt-to-Income ratio must not exceed 40%
- Employment stability must be at least 2 years

4. Early Warning Signals:
- Multiple missed payments within 6 months
- Sudden drop in credit score
- High credit utilization (>80%)

5. Recovery Actions:
- Soft reminder after 7 days delay
- Legal notice after 60 days
- Account marked NPA after 90 days

ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) GUIDELINES

1. KYC Requirements:
- Government-issued ID mandatory
- Address verification required
- PAN card mandatory for Indian customers

2. Suspicious Transaction Monitoring:
Transactions exceeding ₹10,00,000 must be flagged.
Frequent large cash deposits must be investigated.

3. Reporting Requirements:
Suspicious transactions must be reported to regulatory authority within 7 days.

4. Risk-Based Monitoring:
High-risk customers require enhanced due diligence.
Quarterly review is mandatory for high-risk accounts.


DATA SECURITY POLICY

1. Encryption Standards:
All customer data must be encrypted using AES-256.

2. Access Control:
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for admin access.
Role-based access control must be implemented.

3. Audit Logs:
Audit logs must be retained for minimum 5 years.

4. Data Breach Policy:
Any suspected breach must be reported within 24 hours.
Incident response team must investigate within 48 hours.

FRAUD RISK MANAGEMENT

1. Fraud Indicators:
- Multiple transactions from different geographic locations within 1 hour
- High-value transactions outside usual spending pattern
- Repeated OTP failures

2. Automated Blocking:
Accounts showing high-risk fraud patterns must be temporarily blocked.

3. Manual Review:
Fraud analysts must review flagged accounts within 12 hours.

4. Customer Notification:
Customers must be notified immediately in case of suspicious activity.
